Welcome to the April issue of the GI BI and Data Analytics newsletter.
In this issue we are going back to the archives to highlight a great piece of analysis that has been a critical enabler for Project Symphony.
The application of machine learning clustering techniques in the Specification Alignment project introduced a new way of approaching a complex business problem and has unlocked significant potential benefits for the business.

If you would like to know more about any items mentioned in the newsletter, please reach out to PForde@glanbia.com

 

Driving a Data Culture in GI Logistics


GI Logistics have set out an ambitious programme to consolidate, categorize and enhance all of their reporting dashboards for transport, warehousing and exports.

 
 

Machine Learning in Project Symphony


As part of Project Symphony, the GBS Data Analytics team used machine learning techniques to help rationalize the number of bespoke customer material specifications across all of Glanbia Ireland's manufactured materials.

 
 

Providing insight on the thorny issue of interest charges


Agri Finance Business partners and GBS Data Analytics team have developed an analytical model to facilitate the generation and analysis of multiple scenarios around Glanbia Ireland credit policy changes.
