Issue #8

Welcome to the September/October issue of the Tirlán BI and Data Analytics newsletter, in which we focus on the strengthening data culture within Tirlán.

This ongoing evolution in data culture is evidenced through the updates provided by Tirlán’s cross functional community forum, the range and sophistication of solutions being requested by the business, and the adoption metrics we are seeing after a data solution goes live.

We also provide an update on external feedback from SAP and other SAP customers about how Tirlán is leveraging SAP Analytics Cloud (SAC) to meet its information needs.
